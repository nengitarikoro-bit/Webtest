Water Based Activities

Water-based activities all take place on Lochquarry itself.

KAYAKING

Have a go at paddling, rolling, and rafting in one of our brand new kayaks. Max group size 8. Ages 8+

CANOEING

Work single-handedly or in pairs to canoe the length of Lochquarry. Max group size 8 boats. Ages 6+

POWERBOATING

Take control of one of the Centre’s two RIBs and try your hand at powerboating. Max group size 6. Ages 12+
